🔄 Periodic SEO Tasks
Recurring habits that keep this site growing:
📅 Weekly (10 min)
  1. Paste fresh GSC data here, scan Quick Wins & Almost-Ranking sections.
  2. Pick the top 1–2 pages and rewrite their title + meta description for higher CTR or stronger keyword match.
  3. Add 2–3 internal links to those pages from related blog posts.
📆 Monthly (30 min)
  1. Re-run node scripts/generate-pseo.js to refresh lastmod dates in sitemap.
  2. Write 1 new blog post answering a real question from your top GSC queries.
  3. Check Coverage report for index errors; fix any.
  4. Audit broken links / 404s in _redirects.
🗓️ Quarterly (2 hours)
  1. Add new dimensions to programmatic SEO (states, professions, year-specific).
  2. Refresh "year" mentions in old blog posts (2026 → 2027 etc).
  3. Reach out to 3–5 finance bloggers with a relevant post for backlink opportunities.
  4. Submit calculator to 2–3 directory sites (Product Hunt, FinTools, BetaList, etc.).
